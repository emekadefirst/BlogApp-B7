NHRC Calls for Swift Trials of Arrested Ansaru Leaders

By DailyMail Reporter | August 20, 2025 | Politics

The National Human Rights Commission (NHRC) has called on the federal government to swiftly put every machinery in place to ensure trial of the arrested leaders of the notorious terrorist group, Ansaru, ensuring they are held accountable for their crimes.

The call was made on Tuesday by the Executive Secretary of the Commission, Dr. Tony Ojukwu, during the presentation of July 2025 Human Rights Dashboard in Abuja.

Meanwhile, the Nigeria Union of Journalists (NUJ) and International Organisation for Migration (IOM) commended the government for the capture and rehabilitation programs for ex-fighters.

However, the Department of State Services (DSS) cautioned the court against granting bail to suspects linked to the Owo church attack, citing security concerns.
